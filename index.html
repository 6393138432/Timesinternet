About the Masterclass

Unlock the potential of analytics to transform your HR practices and advance your organization’s strategic goals. HR today must provide data-driven insights on implementing and executing strategy through people. In this excel-based, one-day masterclass by Dr. Fermin Diez, you'll immerse yourself hands-on to turn data into actionable insights in HR. Dr. Fermin divides the session in four parts:

  • 1

    Basics of HR Analytics: Get grounded in the statistics, tools, and financial concepts you need to turn HR metrics into business insights.

  • 2

    Translating Analytics into Business Value: Learn how to persuasively present your data-driven HR solutions, ensuring not just insight but also implementation.

  • 3

    Application to a Real-world HR Challenge: Delve into a real HR issue, applying analytical thinking to find data-driven solutions.

  • 4

    HR Analytics for All: Apply the concepts learned in excel, which is a tool everyone already knows how to use! And also learn how to do analytics using ChatGPT4.

What You Will Learn

This is your comprehensive guide to harnessing the power of HR analytics to drive organizational success. Upon completion, participants will:

  • Gain hands-on experience in HR analytics tools and methodologies
  • Link HR policies and practices to organizational performance metrics
  • Master data collection, clean-up, and storage to ensure data validity and reliability
  • Develop predictive models to solve complex HR challenges...
  • Get insights into storytelling and visualization as key elements of the People Analytics toolkit
  • Learn to communicate analytical insights in a business context, enhancing your role as a strategic HR partner

Masterclass Expert

Dr.Fermin Diez

Dr.Fermin Diez

PhD, CCP, SPHR
Dr.Fermin Diez is Deputy CEO and Group Head of Human Resources for Singapore’s National Council for Social Service. He is also an Adjunct Professor at Singapore Management University, at Nanyang Business Schools and at NUS Business School. Fermin has also co-authored three books.

Vast and varied experience in HR, including consulting, corporate and academic roles in more than 40 countries in all continents. As an HR leader, He have been Head of HR for two large MNCs in AsiaPac and in Latin America. Currently, at the National Council of Social Service in Singapore, He aim to improve the capabilities and capacity of the Social Service sector in the country. He is an Adjunct Professor of Human Resources at NUS, SMU and at NTU. He have co-authored three books: ‘Human Capital and Global Business Strategy’, 'The Remuneration Handbook' and ‘Fundamentals of HR Analytics’. He have also taught Business Policy and Strategic Planning at the Graduate Level.
